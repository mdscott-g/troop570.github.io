The best way to learn about Troop 570 is to visit us at one of our troop meetings. Visiting boys can join a patrol for the evening, learn about how our meetings work, and play games with us. Visiting parents can meet with an adult leader to ask questions about our troop. Contact our troop committee chairperson Paul Schindler at paschind@outlook.com to arrange a date to visit.

If you have any other questions, comments, or suggestions for our Troop, please send email to Paul Schindler, Troop Committee Chair at paschind@outlook.com
