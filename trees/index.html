Boy Scout Troop 570 will do curbside pickup of your natural Christmas trees within our designated service area in Kirkland, Washington (map shown below).

Option 1: Curbside pickup

  • Place your natural tree at curbside.
  • We cannot accept flocked or artificial trees. Please remove anything that could clog the chipper (decoration, tinsel, nails, etc)

Option 2: Drop off at McAuliffe Park

  • Saturday and Sunday, January 3rd-4th, 2015 (8am to 4pm)
  • McAuliffe Park is at 10824 NE 116th St, Kirkland, WA 98034

Suggested Donation: $15

  • Please leave cash or check donations payable to "BSA Troop 570" in an envelope (inside a plastic bag if raining).
  • Attach the donation to the tree OR mail to Troop 570, 10021 NE 124th, Kirkland, 98034

Details

  • Trees will be mulched and recycled by the Kirkland Parks Department
  • If we missed your tree by Sunday, please call us at 425-429-8669 and we will pick it up as soon as possible.
  • No calls can be accepted after 3:00pm on Sunday.
